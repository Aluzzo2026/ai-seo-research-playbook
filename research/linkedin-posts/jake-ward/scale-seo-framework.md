\# Research Insight: Jake Ward - Scale SEO Framework



\## Overview

Jake Ward (Founder of Content Growth) is famous for executing aggressive SEO velocity strategies. He focuses on dominating search engines by publishing high-quality, AI-assisted content at massive scale to capture absolute topical authority within a specific niche quickly.



\## Key Insights \& Frameworks



\### 1. Topical Authority Clustering

\- Google no longer ranks isolated keywords; it ranks websites that cover an entire topic exhaustively.

\- \*\*Action:\*\* Create a massive content map. If you are targeting a niche, you must write the pillar page and every single variation, sub-topic, and long-tail question associated with it to show search bots that your site is the ultimate subject matter expert.



\### 2. The AI Efficiency Pipeline

\- Jake proves that AI can match or beat human writing speeds and maintain high quality \*if\* guided properly.

\- Use AI to generate highly technical, in-depth outlines and first drafts based on winning competitor structures.

\- \*\*The Human Touch Rule:\*\* AI does 70% of the heavy lifting (data gathering, structural writing, initial definitions), while human editors do the remaining 30% (adding real-world experience, internal links, brand tone, and custom screenshots) before publishing.



\### 3. Content Velocity as a Competitive Moat

\- Publishing 100 high-quality articles a month beats publishing 5 articles a month because it forces search engine crawlers to re-index your site constantly.

\- This velocity builds topical trust at an accelerated rate, leaving slow-moving competitors behind.



\## Summary for Playbook

To win with AI SEO, shift from slow manual content creation to an assembly-line model. Map the entire topical cluster, leverage AI to scale out production velocity, and aggressively interlink the ecosystem to capture maximum market share in record time.
