\# Research Insight: Ian Nuttall - Programmatic SEO \& AI Automation



\## Overview

Ian Nuttall is a prominent programmatic SEO practitioner and founder known for building high-traffic websites at scale. His core methodology revolves around combining structured databases, LLM APIs, and automation workflows to generate thousands of search-optimized pages efficiently.



\## Key Insights \& Frameworks



\### 1. The Core of Programmatic SEO (pSEO)

\- Programmatic SEO is not about spinning low-quality content; it is about creating landing pages at scale based on structured data that answers low-competition, long-tail search queries.

\- \*\*Formula:\*\* `\[Key Phrase] + \[Database Variable]`. For example: \*"Best AI tool for \[Industry]"\* or \*"How to connect \[Software A] to \[Software B]"\*.



\### 2. AI Content Generation at Scale (API-Driven)

\- Instead of using standard AI writing tools manually, Ian advocates using automated scripts or workflow builders (like n8n or Make) connected directly to OpenAI/Anthropic APIs.

\- To prevent AI hallucinations on large-scale sites, you must feed the prompt a strict database structure (CSV/Airtable) so the AI only writes based on confirmed facts, not imagination.



\### 3. Site Structure and Indexation Strategy

\- Creating 10,000 pages is easy, but getting Google to index them is the real challenge in the AI era.

\- \*\*Internal Linking Grid:\*\* Build a bulletproof internal linking architecture (HTML sitemaps, category hubs, and dynamic contextual links) so Google bots can discover every programmatic page within 2–3 clicks from the homepage.

\- Prioritize clean semantic HTML and fast loading speeds over heavy JavaScript elements.



\## Summary for Playbook

To build successful programmatic SEO sites, focus on unique data curation first. Use automated AI pipelines to format and explain that data, and ensure a robust internal linking grid to guarantee mass indexation by search engine crawlers.
